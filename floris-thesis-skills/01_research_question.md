# Research question

Main research question:
Is there seasonal variation in the spatial extremal dependence of extreme wind gusts at KNMI station locations in the Netherlands?

Interpretation:
This thesis investigates whether extreme wind gust events in the Netherlands are more spatially widespread in some seasons than in others, especially whether winter gust extremes show stronger spatial extremal dependence than summer gust extremes.

Primary comparison:
Winter versus summer.

Primary variable:
FX, hourly maximum wind gust.

Not part of the main thesis:
- Hourly mean wind speed as a separate main variable
- Forecasting wind gusts
- Compound hazards such as hail, precipitation, or storm surge
- General windstorm-risk modelling 
- Vine copulas as the main method, unless explicitly reintroduced later
