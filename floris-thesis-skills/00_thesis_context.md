Thesis topic:
Seasonal variation in spatial extremal dependence of extreme wind gusts at KNMI station locations in the Netherlands

Main research question:
Is there seasonal variation in the spatial extremal dependence of extreme wind speed and wind gusts at KNMI station locations in the Netherlands?

Data:
Hourly KNMI wind speed and wind gust observations, approximately 1951–2026, around 60 stations by 2026, with an unbalanced station network over time, including missing observations.

Central empirical idea:
Test whether extreme wind events are more spatially widespread in winter than in summer.

Important complication:
The station network changes over time, so balanced station subsets, sample splitting, and robustness checks are important.
