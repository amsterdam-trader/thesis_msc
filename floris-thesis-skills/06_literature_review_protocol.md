When reviewing a paper, always answer:

1. What problem does the paper solve?
2. What model or estimator does it use?
3. What assumptions matter?
4. Is it relevant to seasonal spatial extremal dependence of Dutch wind?
5. How could it be used in the thesis?
6. Should it be central, supporting, or excluded?
