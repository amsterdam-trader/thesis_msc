\section*{Data}
The empirical analysis will use the KNMI near real-time 10-minute station data for the Netherlands. I will focus primarily on wind-related variables (e.g. mean wind speed, maximum gust, wind direction) and, if feasible, one additional hazard variable such as precipitation. For reproducibility, the empirical sample will be fixed to a closed time window rather than treated as a live dataset. An initial version of the thesis will likely focus on a subset of representative stations before considering a wider spatial extension.
