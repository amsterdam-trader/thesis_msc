\section*{Main Research Question}
\textbf{How can high-frequency KNMI observations be represented as functional windstorm-event curves and combined with extreme-value and copula models to quantify the joint behaviour of extreme windstorm characteristics in the Netherlands?}

The thesis will focus on four sub-questions:
\begin{enumerate}
    \item How should windstorm events be extracted from 10-minute KNMI data in a statistically and physically meaningful way?
    \item Which functional summaries best capture within-event wind dynamics?
    \item How well do EVT models describe the tails of key storm characteristics such as peak wind speed, duration, and cumulative severity?
    \item What dependence structure exists between these characteristics and, where feasible, across selected stations?
\end{enumerate}
